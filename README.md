# 🌍 A.R.I.S – Atmospheric Risk Intelligence System

## Overview

A.R.I.S (Atmospheric Risk Intelligence System) is an AI-powered urban air quality intelligence platform designed to help authorities monitor, analyze, predict, and mitigate air pollution in smart cities.

The system combines environmental data, AI-driven source attribution, AQI forecasting, intervention planning, and impact simulation into a single operational dashboard.

---

## Problem Statement

Urban air pollution is a complex challenge caused by multiple factors such as traffic congestion, construction activities, industrial emissions, and weather conditions.

Traditional monitoring systems only report pollution levels but fail to answer critical questions:

* Why is pollution increasing?
* What is the primary source?
* What actions should authorities take?
* What impact will those actions create?

A.R.I.S addresses these gaps through closed-loop decision intelligence.

---

## Key Features

### 📊 Real-Time Air Quality Monitoring

* Live AQI tracking across multiple zones
* Risk classification (Moderate, High, Severe)
* Zone-wise environmental metrics

### 🧠 AI Source Attribution Engine

Identifies pollution contributors using weighted analysis:

* Traffic Emissions
* Construction Activities
* Industrial Emissions

### 🔮 Hyperlocal AQI Forecasting

* 6-Hour AQI Prediction
* 12-Hour AQI Prediction
* Confidence Scoring System

### 🎯 AI Intervention Planning

Generates recommended actions such as:

* Traffic Diversion
* Construction Suspension
* Industrial Inspection

### 📈 Impact Simulation

Simulates the expected AQI reduction before implementing interventions.

### 🗺️ Geospatial Intelligence Dashboard

* Interactive zone visualization
* Pollution hotspot mapping
* Satellite-inspired monitoring interface
